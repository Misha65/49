Interesting facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication between chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for incredible navigation abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about pandas

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Pandas' eyes are different to normal bears.
  • Pandas can swim.

Interesting facts about Teddy bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • Before teddy bears could be sold, Michtom had to get President Roosevelt's permission to use his name.

Interesting facts about flying cicadas

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.
  • Humans can eat cicadas.
  • Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism.
